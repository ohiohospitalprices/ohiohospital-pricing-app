## CANVA QUICK BUILD (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Create New Video
- Click **"Create a design"** → **"Video"**
- Select **1080 x 1920** (TikTok vertical format)

### Step 2: Upload Images
- Click **"Uploads"** in left menu
- Drag all 13 images from `C:\Users\Owner\Desktop\TikTok_Resized` into Canva
- Upload them in bulk

### Step 3: Build Timeline
1. Add first image (`download hearse_resized.jpg`)
2. Set duration to **5 seconds** (bottom of timeline)
3. Add second image, set to 5 sec
4. Continue with rest of images in order from table above
5. Each image gets its duration from the "Duration" column

### Step 4: Add Audio
1. Click **"Audio"** in left menu
2. Click **"Upload"** → Select your ElevenLabs MP3 file
3. Drag audio track to timeline
4. Trim/sync so it aligns with video (should be ~90 sec)

### Step 5: Preview & Export
1. Click **"Preview"** to watch the full video
2. Check that:
   - Images match the narration
   - Audio is synced
   - No gaps or overlaps
3. Click **"Download"** → Select **MP4** format
4. Choose **"Download"** (this creates the final video file)
